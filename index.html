Do Dragons have an effect on winning League of Legends Games?

Dragons? Winning?

In the game League of Legends there is a clear goal, to destory the enemy's nexus and win the game. There are many factors in winning a game, whether it be your team has the higher Creep Score (cs) which leads to more gold which leads to better items helping you win the game, or you get the gold from getting more kills, or you get the gold from taking objectives such as the towers. Or even if you're behind you can win by slaying all of the possible elemental dragons that spawn throughout the game. However it may be, there are many ways to win a match, in this project we will be taking a dive into the importance of Elemental Drakes (Dragons) and Dragons in general when it comes to winning a game. Our dataset involves match data from various pro leagues during the 2022 season. The main columns we will be looking at are the columns: {'elementaldrakes','dragons','opp_dragons','result','elders'}. The first three columns contain the number of dragons each team has including 'elders' which is a special dragon, and result which is the result of the game.
